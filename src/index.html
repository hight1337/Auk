About us

Since 2003, “AEC” LLC (stands for “agricultural engineering company”) was fulfilling Russian agricultural needs with World's best-in-class technological solutions. Our core business approach is based on the provision of innovations in various segments of agricultural machinery and equipment. Besides, in our daily activities, we imply top-notch international expertise to offer the best support, integration, and facilitation services to our clients.

Through years of hard work, dedication, and research we have managed to build up an extensive bank of experience in a multitude of segments ranging from irrigation systems, hose dragging, potato storages and vegetable handling equipment, to solutions in animal-by-products processing and production of granular organic and organic-mineral fertilizers. The broad and successful track record, combined with a wide network of international partnerships enabled our company to differentiate itself as a market disruptor, trendsetter, and engineering innovation pioneer on the Russian market landscape.
